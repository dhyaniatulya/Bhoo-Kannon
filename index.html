Understanding Bhoo Kanoon: Protecting Land Rights

Bhoo Kanoon governs land laws in Uttarakhand, ensuring protection for local communities and sustainable development.

Impact In Uttarakhand without law

Bhoo Kanoon plays a crucial role in safeguarding the rights of landowners and promoting sustainable land use. It is essential for all citizens to understand their rights and responsibilities under these laws to ensure fair practices and equitable development in Uttarakhand.

Landslides in Uttarakhand

Landslides pose a significant risk in Uttarakhand, impacting communities, infrastructure, and the environment. Awareness and preparedness are essential to mitigate these risks.
