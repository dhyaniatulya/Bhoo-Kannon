In Uttarakhand, people from outside the state are easily purchasing land for investment purposes without any limit. Uttarakhand land law is very flexible due to the abolition of the maximum limit on land purchase. Outsiders are taking advantage of the poor people of Uttarakhand and purchasing the land at a very cheap price. Due to this, the culture, food habits, dress, and lifestyle of the Pahadi people are changing and slowly disappearing. The land law actually sets limits on the purchase of land by outsiders, so the culture of Uttarakhand will remain the same and the people will not become landless in their own state.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about the importance of land laws in Uttarakhand and how they affect local communities. We believe that by empowering individuals with the right knowledge, we can ensure fair practices, protect land rights, and promote responsible land management that supports long-term development goals for the region.
